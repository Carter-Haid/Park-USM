About Us

Everything there is to know about the Campus Parking Project




The Campus Parking Project story began in January of 2019. Frustrated by the parking management methods used at the University of Southern Maine, Carter Haid and Connor Black set out to create a full parking management system that could be used by anyone.

Since 2019 Carter has continued to develop the project while Connor has taken an advisory role... mainly focusing on keeping the HTML/CSS within material design guidelines. Several other developers have written small scripts for the project all under the management of Carter.

Some positions that we're looking for in 2021:
- Graphic Designer (don't look now but... we don't have logos.)
- Traffic Engineering Consultant

If you'd like to collaborate on the project feel free to reach out or make a pull request on GitHub. To collaborate in a different way please feel free to reach out via our contact form. We're looking forward to 2021 and to the future of Campus Parking Project. Have a great and safe 2021.
