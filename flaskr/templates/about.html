About Us

Everything there is to know about the Campus Parking Project.

The Campus Parking Project story began in December of 2019. Frustrated by the parking management methods used at the University of Southern Maine, Carter Haid and Connor Black set out to create an open source parking management system that could be easily implemented by anyone.

Since 2019 Carter has continued to develop the project while Connor has taken an advisory role... mainly focusing on keeping the HTML/CSS within material design guidelines. Several other developers have written small scripts for the project all under the management of Carter.

If you'd like to collaborate on the project feel free to make a pull request on GitHub. To collaborate in a different way please feel free to reach out via our contact form. We're looking forward to the new year and to the future of Campus Parking Project. Have a great and safe 2021.
